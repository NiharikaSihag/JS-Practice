Module

PMAI101 Product Thinking & Customer Insight

This module introduces the fundamentals of product management with a focus on user-centered thinking. Learners will explore how to identify user needs, conduct problem discovery, and define value propositions. Tools such as personas, journey maps, and MVP scoping will be applied through a structured product discovery process.

Important Topics:

  • Role of a Product Manager
  • Jobs-to-Be-Done framework
  • Problem-Solution Fit
  • User research & persona creation
  • MVP definition
  • Opportunity sizing
  • Product vision & roadmap overview
  • Value proposition design

Project: Define a product opportunity and create a detailed discovery document with personas, problem statements, and MVP features.

Module

PMAI102 Building & Managing Digital Products

This module covers the end-to-end lifecycle of building and managing digital products. Learners will explore product development methodologies, agile processes, sprint planning, stakeholder management, and success metrics. Emphasis will be on aligning product execution with customer feedback and business goals.

Important Topics:

  • Agile & Scrum fundamentals
  • Product development lifecycle
  • Feature prioritization techniques
  • Product analytics & KPIs
  • Managing cross-functional teams
  • Stakeholder alignment & communication
  • Go-to-market strategy
  • Product iteration based on feedback

Project: Design a product roadmap and execution strategy using agile principles and stakeholder inputs for a live digital product.

Module

PMAI201 Introduction to Agentic AI & Intelligent Systems

This module introduces core concepts of agentic AI systems and intelligent product experiences. Students will learn about LLMs, prompt engineering, autonomous agents, and ethical implications of AI in products. The focus is on understanding how intelligent systems can enhance user journeys and automate decision-making.

Important Topics:

  • Introduction to agentic AI
  • Capabilities of LLMs (Large Language Models)
  • Prompt engineering techniques
  • Designing autonomous agents
  • Human-AI collaboration
  • Ethical & responsible AI
  • Real-world AI product examples
  • Evaluating AI system performance

Project: Build a functional agent prototype using LLMs that performs a task like search, summarization, or automation based on user prompts.

Projects: Students will work on module-specific projects that integrate product strategy and AI-driven workflows while developing AI agents for real-world applications.
